Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) is a community supported by Google for students of IERT interested in Google developer technologies, irrespective of their domain, with an interest in growing as a developer. By joining GDSC IERT, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community. Google Developer Student Clubs are dedicated to helping peers to learn and connect. GDSC IERT organises workshop for learning, networking, and growing together as a community.
